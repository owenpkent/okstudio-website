About OKStudio

OKStudio was founded by Owen Kent, a drone pilot with over seven years of experience who uses aerial technology to overcome the limitations of his wheelchair and explore the world from new perspectives. A graduate of the film school at UC Berkeley, Owen combines his cinematic eye with technical expertise to create breathtaking aerial visuals. Proficient in both DJI and ArduPilot systems, he has a passion for photogrammetry, mapping, and cinematic aerial videography. Owen leads a team of certified drone pilots and aerial photography experts dedicated to capturing stunning perspectives that tell your story from above. With state-of-the-art equipment and the expertise to handle any mission, no project is too big or too small for OKStudio to deliver exceptional results.
